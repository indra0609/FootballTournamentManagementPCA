Our Mission

Our mission is to create a fair and organized tournament platform where teams can compete with passion and discipline. We encourage youth participation, teamwork, fitness, and a strong sporting spirit in Parbatipur and surrounding regions.

About This Application

  • Public users can view teams, players, matches, and results.
  • Admin users can manage tournament data (Teams, Players, Matches).
  • All data is stored in Google Sheets (no backend server used).
  • Designed for short-term tournament usage (3 months).
